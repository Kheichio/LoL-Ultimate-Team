LoL Ultimate Team

Build your dream League of Legends roster. Open card packs, sign the best pros, grind Season Split matches, and compete in tournaments — from the local Gaming Cafe all the way to the World Championship.

Getting Started — Your First Steps

1

Open Your Free Starter Pack

Go to the Store tab. At the top you'll see a free Starter Pack — click it to claim your first six cards: one player per role (TOP, JNG, MID, ADC, SUP) plus a Head Coach. These form your starter lineup.

2

Build Your Starting Squad

Head to the Squad tab and slot your starter cards into each role. You need at minimum a TOP, JNG, MID, ADC, and SUP to enter tournaments. A Coach is optional but boosts your coaching stats.

3

Play the Gaming Cafe Tournament

Hit PLAY and enter the Gaming Cafe Tournament — the entry-level bracket and completely free to enter. Win rounds to earn Blue Essence (BE), the currency used for everything in the game.

4

Play Season Split Matches

Open the Season tab — this is your main long-term loop. Each split has 10 generated opponents; every match is Best of 5 rounds where you pick stat-based plays against them. Watch for team metas (stat buffs) and team slumps (stat penalties) affecting real teams your cards belong to. Heads up: once you play your first match in a split, your squad locks until the split ends — pick your lineup carefully. Splits reward BE, manager XP, and unlock further tournament tiers.

5

Open Packs & Upgrade Your Roster

Spend your BE in the Store on card packs to pull new players. Standard packs are cheapest; higher packs yield rarer cards. Better-rated cards win more matches and unlock bigger tournaments. You can also use the Upgrade Lab — combine cards of the same role and tier to forge a higher-tier card (10 cards for most tiers, just 5 for Master→Grandmaster and Grandmaster→Challenger).

6

Climb the Tournament Ladder & Special Modes

Gaming Cafe and Regional Split are open from the start. First Stand unlocks once you complete a Playoff-tier (6+ win) Season Split. MSI unlocks after winning First Stand and completing 2 total splits. Worlds unlocks after winning MSI and completing 3 total splits. Draft Mode unlocks early — just win 1 Regional Split and hit Playoff tier once. Take on the Golden Road gauntlet to conquer all standard tournament stages in a single run (30-min cooldown between runs). Try the Salary Cap Draft — build a roster under a salary budget for creative deckbuilding. Or enter the Infinite Tower — an endless free-entry gauntlet with scaling opponents and permanent buffs after each win. Checkpoints every 10 floors save your progress.

Quick Tips

  • Check Quests often — they award large chunks of BE for milestones you're already hitting, plus live Achievements for squad rating and Archive progress.
  • The Trade Market refreshes every 15 min with high-tier cards you can acquire using fodder cards.
  • Your Squad cards are protected from selling and trades — only Club cards can be liquidated. Remember: it locks once you start a Season Split.
  • Manage your Skills tree to unlock scouting bonuses, tactical edges, cheaper Bootcamp cooldowns, and faster XP.

📊 Player Stats Explained

MEC
Mechanics — Individual mechanical skill ceiling. High MEC players dominate in Aggression-focused draft phases.
TMF
Teamfight — Coordination in team skirmishes. Essential for Teamfight draft phase and synergy with supports.
MAP
Map Awareness — Vision control, rotations, and objective priority. Governs Objective draft phase advantage.
FRM
Form — Current in-form peak performance. Coaches have very low FRM by design (staff, not players).
CMP
Composure — Mental fortitude under pressure. High CMP players perform consistently even in deficit positions.
LDR
Leadership — Shot-calling ability and team morale impact. Particularly impactful on Coaches and support players.
Overall Rating is a weighted average of all 6 stats. Out-of-position penalties apply: placing a player outside their designated role deducts -20 rating from their contribution.

⚔️ Match & Tournament Mechanics

Tournaments are bracket-style gauntlets. Each round pits your squad against a CPU opponent with a set power rating.

Tactical Draft Phase Before each match, choose a focus stat: Aggression (MEC), Teamfight (TMF), or Objective (MAP). Your avg stat vs enemy avg determines a Power modifier. Choose the stat where you have the biggest edge.
Power Formula Total Power = Avg Rating + Region Chem + Year Chem + Coach Bonus (scales with coach rating) + Elite Pedigree + Training Bonus + Tactical Modifier + Manager Tactics Skill
Match Resolution A random variance of ±7 is applied at resolution. Matches are never certain — a power advantage improves your odds but upsets happen.

🏆 Tournament Modes

Gaming Cafe — 10 BE

3 rounds · Silver-tier opponents · 1st: 100 BE · 2nd: 20 BE. Easiest mode — ideal for brand-new rosters and early grind.

Regional Split — 100 BE

3 rounds · Silver / Platinum opponents · 1st: 400 BE · 2nd: 100 BE. First real challenge; Platinum players appear in later rounds.

First Stand — 200 BE

3 rounds · Platinum / Diamond opponents · 1st: 800 BE · 2nd: 200 BE. Requires a Diamond-level roster to reliably win.

MSI Bracket — 350 BE

3 rounds · Master / Grandmaster opponents · 1st: 1,600 BE · 2nd: 400 BE. High difficulty — needs Master-tier stars.

World Championship — 600 BE

4 rounds · Challenger-tier opponents · 1st: 3,000 BE · 2nd: 800 BE. Hardest standalone mode — bring your absolute best.

🌟 Golden Road — 2,500 BE

6-stage gauntlet (Regional Split × 3 → First Stand → MSI Arena → World Championship). 19 total matches, must win every stage. Massive BE bonus on completion. The rarest achievement in the game. 30-minute cooldown between runs, whether you complete the gauntlet or get knocked out.

🎯 Draft Mode — 1,000 BE

🔒 Unlocks after winning 1 Regional Split and completing a Playoff-tier Season Split. Requires 15 club cards. Ban phase (up to 5 each side) → Role Assignment (assign cards to TOP/JNG/MID/ADC/SUP + optional COACH) → 3-round match. R1 & R2: pick MEC/TMF/MAP focus. R3 Hybrid: pick tactical stat then fundamentals stat, average decides. CPU difficulty scales to your card pool rating. Win: 5,000 BE · Runner-up (1W): 1,500 BE · 0W: no payout.

🏆 Season Splits — Free Entry

Accessed from Play → Open Season. A split consists of 10 opponents generated at the start with scaling difficulty — Match 1 is the easiest, Match 10 is the hardest. You can play them in any order, one at a time; progress saves between sessions. Only Season Match results count toward your split record — standard tournaments do not. Winning or losing a match awards manager XP (+75 / +25, multiplied by Mentorship).

How a match works: Each match is Best of 5 rounds. Every round you choose one of three randomly offered plays — each play pits a specific set of your players' stats against the opponent's matching stat. The edge is shown upfront (e.g. +7 or −3) with a random ±8 variance applied on resolution. First to 3 round wins takes the match.

Team Metas & Slumps: 5–10 real teams get a random +8 to +25 buff to one specific stat — if you have a card from that team, plays using that stat get a significant boost. Separately, 5–15 other real teams are in a slump, taking a flat −8 to −15 penalty across all stats. These reroll once mid-split — right after your 5th match — so the back half of every split brings a fresh set of metas and slumps. Both are shown on the Season Matches screen and the Season tab, including exactly which of your current squad members are affected.

Elite Splits: 20% chance through Split 4, then rising 2% per split from Split 5 onward (capped at 70%) to keep late-game progression interesting. Elite splits upgrade 1 to 6 CPU opponents to a 95–110 rating with fully regenerated stats — 110 is the ultimate difficulty ceiling, well above any player card. Flagged with 🔥 wherever they appear.

Opponent styles skew their stats: Aggressive teams spike MEC, Defensive teams spike MAP/LDR, Chaotic teams have extreme MEC but weak CMP. Split rewards on completion: 10W = 8,000 BE · 8–9W = 5,000 · 6–7W = 3,000 · 4–5W = 1,500 · 0–3W = 500. All completed splits are logged in the Season tab trophy case, alongside a live Split Standings chart ranking your team against simulated CPU records.

🔒 Tournament Progression

Gaming Cafe and Regional Split are always open. First Stand unlocks after completing a Season Split at Playoff tier or better (6+ wins). MSI unlocks after winning First Stand and completing 2 total Season Splits. Worlds unlocks after winning MSI and completing 3 total Season Splits. Draft Mode unlocks after winning 1 Regional Split and completing a Playoff-tier (6+ win) Season Split — kept early so you don't have to wait until Worlds to try it.

Difficulty scales by +3 per round plus a random 0–3 variance, so later rounds of each mode push into the next tier bracket.

💼 Manager Skills

🔭 Scouting Network

Each level adds +0.25% to every pack RNG roll. At level 5: +1.25% — a meaningful nudge without distorting rare drop rates.

🎯 Tactical Acumen

Grants +3 guaranteed power per level in the Tactical Draft phase. At level 5: +15 flat power — equivalent to a full chemistry bonus.

🔀 Transfer Window

Expands and sweetens the Exchange Hub market. Lv1: 25% chance per trade offer for a Transfer Window Discount (~25% off asset cost). Lv2: 4 offers per refresh. Lv3: Unlocks the Role Filter to target specific positions. Lv4: 5 offers. Lv5: Force Refresh locks the entire market to your chosen role.

🏃 Team Conditioning

Reduces the cooldown between Season Matches. Lv1: 45s. Lv2: 30s. Lv3: 20s. Lv4: 10s. Lv5: No cooldown — play back to back.

🎓 Mentorship Program

Multiplies all manager XP gains from every source. Lv1: +25%. Lv2: +50%. Lv3: +75%. Lv4: ×2 (double XP). Lv5: ×2.5 — reach new levels and skill points significantly faster.

🏋️ Bootcamp Director

Costs double Skill Points per level compared to other skills. Increases the Bootcamp power bonus by +2 per level — from +5 base up to +15 at level 5, matching what used to require expensive higher BE tiers.

Earn XP by opening packs (+25 XP) and winning matches (+100 XP). Each level up grants 1 Skill Point. Skills cost escalating SP: level 1→1 SP, level 2→2 SP, etc.

🎯 Draft Mode Guide

Requires 15 club cards & 1,000 BE entry. CPU difficulty scales to your card pool rating.
Ban Phase — Click CPU pool cards to ban up to 5. CPU auto-bans 5 from your pool.
Role Assignment — Click a role slot (TOP/JNG/MID/ADC/SUP), then click a card to assign. COACH is optional; any card can flex into any slot at −10 rating penalty. Legacy-tier cards are exempt — they flex into any role at full value, marked with a "FLEX ✓" badge instead of the penalty.
Round 1 & 2 — Tactical — Pick MEC (Aggression), TMF (Teamfight), or MAP (Objective). Your team avg + ±7 variance vs CPU avg.
Round 3 — Hybrid Finale — Phase 1: pick from MEC/TMF/MAP. Phase 2: pick from FRM/CMP/LDR. Average of both scores + variance decides the round.
Best of 3 — Win 2 rounds → 2,500 BE. Win 0 or 1 → no payout. Winner takes all.

💰 Salary Cap Draft Guide

1,500 BE entry · Win: 4,000 BE · Loss: No payout. Requires 15 club cards.
Salary Formula — Each card costs (rating − 60) × 2.5 salary units. A 99-rated Challenger costs 97.5; a 75-rated Silver costs 37.5.
Budget Cap: 350 — Fill 5 roles without exceeding budget. No ban phase — go straight to picks. CPU also builds under the same cap.
Combat — Same Best-of-3 tactical system as regular Draft Mode after teams are locked.

🗼 Infinite Tower Guide

Free entry · No unlock required. Uses your current squad. Endless scaling difficulty.
How it works — Each floor is a Bo3 match using the same play-pick-from-3 stat system as Season Matches. Win 2 rounds to advance. Lose 2 and your run ends.
Buffs — After each win, choose 1 of 3 permanent buffs: +5 flat stat, +15% to a stat, +8 role power, or +3 all stats. These stack infinitely to match ever-scaling opponents.
Scaling — Opponents start at rating 72 and gain +2.5 per floor (caps at 130). By floor 20+ you need significant buff stacking to compete.
Checkpoints — Every 10 floors: progress saved + BE reward (2k → 4k → 6k → 8k). Your all-time highest floor is tracked permanently.

⚔️ Summoner's Rift Simulation Guide

A turn-based tactical simulation of a full League of Legends match. Your 5 squad members face a CPU team of real database cards matched to your skill level. Both teams are fully visible with all stats displayed.

How It Works — Each turn, assign actions to your 5 roles via dropdowns. Every action shows the stat check used, your player's value, the CPU opponent's value, and the edge (+EDGE or -RISK) so you can make informed decisions. Click "Execute Turn" to resolve all actions. The battle log names both players and shows exact stat comparisons for every outcome.
Phases — Early (turns 1-8): Farm, Gank, Void Grubs, Herald, Ward. Mid (turns 9-16): + Dragon, Teamfight, Rotate, Split Push. Late (turn 17+): + Baron, Elder Dragon, Siege, Backdoor.
Stat Checks — Farm → FRM. Gank → MEC+MAP. Teamfight → TMF+MEC+LDR. Split Push → MEC+FRM. Dragon/Herald → TMF+MAP. Baron → TMF+MAP+LDR. Siege → all 6 stats. Each check compares your player vs the CPU's matching role player (e.g., your TOP's MEC vs their TOP's MEC).
Objective Progression — Objectives must be taken in order: Void Grubs → Rift Herald → Baron. You can't skip ahead. Dragons require 2+ before Elder is available. Dragon Soul (4/4) locks out further dragon takes. Laners (TOP/MID/SUP) can assist jungle objectives — assign them to the same action to boost your chances.
Objective Buffs — 🐉 Dragons: +3 stats each, 4 = Soul (+8 all). 🟣 Baron: +15 siege for 3 turns (siege destroys 2 structures!). 👁️ Herald: instant tower charge. 🪲 Grubs: +5 push power. 👁️ Vision: +5 to all checks next turn.
Tower Destruction — Each lane has 3 towers → inhibitor → nexus towers (2) → nexus. Must destroy in order. Siege with Baron buff destroys 2 at once. Backdoor only works when inhibitors are down. Destroying the nexus wins the game.
Rewards — Win: 1,500 BE + 200 XP. Loss: 500 BE + 50 XP. Free entry. ~15-25 turns per game. CPU team scales to your squad's average rating.

⚔️ Summoner's Rift Simulation is here! A brand-new turn-based tactical game mode — assign your players to lanes, contest objectives, teamfight, and destroy the enemy nexus. Plus 136 new cards, 11 Worlds Finalist Legacy cards, and a new PCS region. 1,306 cards in the database.
  • Summoner's Rift (New Mode) — Turn-based LoL match simulation. Farm, gank, teamfight, take dragons/baron, siege towers, destroy the nexus. Actions resolve via your card stats. ~15-25 turns per game. Free entry, 1,500 BE win.
  • NA LCS 2015 — 50 cards, 10 teams. TSM (Bjergsen), CLG (Doublelift), C9 (Sneaky/Hai), TL (Piglet 96), TIP (Impact/Rush).
  • LCP 2025 + PCS 2024 — 75 cards. LCP gets 2025 coverage (8 teams). New PCS region with 7 teams and its own Archive tab.
  • Worlds Finalists — KOO Tigers 2015 (Smeb 97, PraY 98) and Samsung Galaxy 2016 (Ruler 94, CoreJJ 92). 11 Legacy cards.
  • Archive Overhaul — Independent rewards per category, search bar, completion notifications.
  • QoL — Global event timer (midnight UTC), leaderboard sorting, Club Capacity 20 levels (1,100 max).
