Rules & Terminology Guide

Master the mechanics of team building and tactical drafting.

1. Player Attributes

MEC (Mechanics)

Raw mechanical skill. Represents dodging, skillshot accuracy, reaction times, and laning phase micro-management.

TMF (Teamfighting)

Target selection, positioning, and execution during late-game 5v5 group fights and objective skirmishes.

FRM (Farming)

Efficiency in CSing (creep score) and wave management. Supports typically have very low FRM stats.

CMP (Composure)

Mental fortitude and clutch factor. Determines how well players perform in high-pressure situations or when playing from behind.

MAP (Map Awareness)

Vision control, tracking enemy jungle pathing, and setting up macro rotations for dragons and barons.

LDR (Leadership)

Shotcalling, macro coordination, and team synergy. Usually highest on veteran players, supports, and coaches.

2. Squad Chemistry

  • Temporal Synergy: Rewards you for using players from the same historic era or competitive year. The fewer unique years on your roster, the higher the bonus (up to +5).
  • Regional Synergy: Rewards you for aligning players from the same competitive region (e.g., LCK, LPL). Stacking players from the exact same Franchise (e.g., all T1 players) provides a bonus +2 modifier (capped at +5).
  • The Champion Wildcard: Specialized Champion cards act as universal chemistry links across eras. They fit seamlessly into any system, bypassing positional/temporal penalties.

3. Tactical Draft Phase

Before every match simulation, you must lock in a Strategic Focus: MEC, TMF, or MAP.

The game calculates your Squad's average score in that chosen stat and compares it to the Opponent's generated stat. Exploiting a weak enemy stat grants your team a massive temporary Combat Power multiplier. However, if you draft into an opponent's core strength, your team will be heavily penalized.

4. Manager Progression & Skills

As you play, you will earn Manager XP for opening packs (+25 XP) and winning tournaments (+100 XP). Each time you level up, you earn a Skill Point.

  • Scouting Network: Upgrading this skill permanently boosts your open luck rate modifiers, significantly increasing your chances of finding high-tier (Diamond, Master, GM, Challenger) cards.
  • Corporate Negotiation: Decreases the baseline inflation penalty whenever you take out Corporate Credit Hub loans.
  • Tactical Acumen: This skill applies a guaranteed flat power bonus (+2 Power per Level) to your squad during the Tactical Draft phase, acting as an unconditional baseline anchor that buffers losing matchups.

5. Card Tiers & Managers

  • Silver: 70-79 OVR
  • Gold: 80-84 OVR
  • Platinum: 85-87 OVR
  • Diamond: 88-91 OVR
  • Master: 92-94 OVR
  • Grandmaster: 95-96 OVR
  • Challenger: 97-99 OVR
  • MVP: Historical standout performers.
  • Champion: World winners. Grants Universal Chemistry links across all eras/regions.
  • Coach Platform: Placed in the MGMT slot. Grants a flat +5 Team Power bonus. Coaches have low Mechanics/Farming but extreme Leadership/Map Awareness to boost your tactical averages.

6. Store Packs Overview

  • Starter Pack: Free one-time claim. Grants 5 base assets to kickstart your club.
  • Standard Pack (400 BE): 5 assets. Great for building early depth with Silver/Gold profiles.
  • Elite Pack (1000 BE): 5 assets. Increases chances for Platinum and Diamond drops.
  • Supreme Pack (2000 BE): High risk, high reward. Unlocks the possibility of pulling Master, GM, and Challenger cards.
  • MVP Pack (4000 BE): Guaranteed Platinum+ floors with an isolated 25% chance to pull an elite MVP veteran.
  • Champions Pack (6000 BE): 1 Guaranteed Champion (Universal Synergy link) and 4 filler cards.
  • Regional Pack (800+ BE): Target specific leagues (LCK, LPL, LEC, LCS) and customize the tier ceiling. Pricing scales dynamically with the chosen maximum tier.
